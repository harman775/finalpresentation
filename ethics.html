Ethical & Social Considerations

Privacy, fairness, and inclusive design principles

The platform prioritizes user privacy, algorithmic fairness, and inclusive design, ensuring responsible data management and ethical decision-making.

Privacy & Control

  • Clear opt-in consent during registration
  • Granular visibility controls for all data fields
  • GDPR-compliant deletion procedures (30 days)
  • Encryption, role-based access, 2FA
  • Regular security audits and breach notification

Algorithm Fairness

  • Testing for demographic parity
  • Proxy-variable bias checks
  • Transparent scoring algorithms
  • Professional review of results
  • User-facing explanations of recommendations

Accessibility

  • WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
  • Screen reader compatibility
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • High contrast modes
  • Inclusive language throughout
  • Mobile-responsive design

Social Responsibility

  • Anti-discriminatory job posting filters
  • No paid tiers - equitable access for all
  • Alumni retain data ownership
  • Energy-efficient cloud platforms
  • Decisions based on fairness and empathy
