Lessons Learned & Team Reflection

Insights from our research journey

The project provided significant learning opportunities in teamwork, technical execution, and ethical decision-making.

01

Scope Management

The broad scope required clear role allocation (data engineering, design, analytics, documentation) for parallel collaboration and accountability. Feature prioritization focused on core MVP functionality rather than trying to build everything at once.

02

Regular Check-ins

Daily check-ins and recurring meetings facilitated early identification of blockers and risks, especially during data cleaning phases. This prevented major setbacks and kept the team aligned.

03

Incremental Validation

Emphasized the critical importance of incremental validation for integration usability and pre-agreeing on data standards. This approach reduced rework and improved final product quality.

04

User Experience Focus

Early prototypes highlighted navigation challenges, leading to revisions with clearer labels, simplified workflows, and common layout patterns based on stakeholder feedback.

05

Data Privacy Balance

Negotiating trade-offs between usability, transparency, and legal compliance for granular consent controls taught that privacy involves balancing user autonomy with institutional interests.

06

Embracing Iteration

Almost every project element underwent multiple iterations as understanding deepened. The final product reflects not only technical execution but also a mature collaborative process.

Team Growth & Impact

The project enhanced technical knowledge in data analytics and system design, alongside valuable insights into teamwork, decision-making, and ethical responsibility. Differences in work approaches fostered adaptability through constructive feedback, compromise, and iterative decision-making.

Project Impact & Next Steps

The GHU Alumni Connect Analytics Platform successfully transforms institutional operations from anecdotal to data-driven decision-making, improving fundraising, employer partnerships, career support, and alumni engagement.
